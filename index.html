Our Humble Beginnings

From our family to yours

What started as a small farm stand selling grandma's famous pies has grown into a beloved neighborhood eatery. In 2012, we converted the old barn on our family property into a cozy 12-table restaurant.

Today, we still use many of the same recipes passed down through generations, now with ingredients sourced from our own gardens and local farmers we've known for decades.

Every dish tells a story - whether it's the heirloom tomatoes from the Johnson farm down the road, or the honey from our neighbor's apiary that sweetens your dessert.

Rooted in community

We believe food tastes better when you know where it comes from and who grew it. That's why we're proud to partner with over 15 local farms and producers within a 20-mile radius.

Our menu changes with the seasons - sometimes even week to week depending on what's fresh and available. Our chefs visit the farmers market every Saturday to plan the week's specials.

When you dine with us, you're not just enjoying a meal - you're supporting a network of small-scale farmers, bakers, and artisans who pour their hearts into what they create.
