A Note About Our Menu

Our menu changes seasonally to showcase the best ingredients available from our local partners. Some items may not be available depending on seasonal availability.

We're committed to supporting local farmers and producers within a 20-mile radius. All our meats are humanely raised, and our produce is organically grown when possible.

Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions or allergies.
