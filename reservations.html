Reservation Policies

Confirmation

All reservations are confirmed within 24 hours via email or phone.

Cancellation

Please give us 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or modify your reservation.

Large Parties

Parties of 8 or more require a phone call to arrange seating and may require a deposit.

Wait Time

We hold tables for 15 minutes past your reservation time before releasing them.

Walk-ins

Walk-ins are welcome and seated based on availability, but reservations are recommended.

Private Events

Looking to host a special event? We offer private dining options for groups of 20-40 people.
